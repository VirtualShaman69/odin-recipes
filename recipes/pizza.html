BBQ chicken Pizza

The Delicious BBQ pizza is one of ower own special recipes crafted from the finest herbs mixed with 3 different cheeses and the finest free range chicken

ingredients

  • tomato base
  • barbequed chicken strips
  • fryed onions
  • parsley
  • spring leaks
  • special sauce
  • garlic
  • dil
  • oregano
  • mozzarella, fetta and Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. roll out dough
  2. spread tomato base accros dough
  3. apply all ingredients on top of dough except cheeses
  4. apply cheese
  5. put raw pizza in wood oven and cook until ready
